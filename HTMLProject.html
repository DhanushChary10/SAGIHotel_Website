About Us

                Sagi 5-Star Hotel: A Legacy of Luxury in India:
                        Welcome to Sagi 5-Star Hotel, an iconic name in luxury hospitality that has been offering unparalleled service and exceptional experiences since its 
                        inception. Situated in some of the most sought-after destinations across India, Sagi 5-Star Hotel is the epitome of luxury, blending timeless elegance 
                        with contemporary sophistication.
                    Our History: A Journey of Excellence:
                        The story of Sagi 5-Star Hotel began in 2005 with the opening of our flagship property in Mumbai, India’s financial capital. The hotel was created with
                        a vision to provide a luxury experience that caters to both business and leisure travelers, with a focus on elegance, comfort, and world-class service.
                        Since then, Sagi 5-Star Hotel has grown into one of the most recognized luxury hotel chains in India, with multiple branches across the country.

                        What started as a boutique luxury hotel has expanded its reach, with locations in major metropolitan cities, each reflecting the unique charm of 
                        the region while maintaining the signature opulence and excellence that Sagi is known for.

                    Our Vision: Luxury Beyond Expectations:
                        The founding vision of Sagi 5-Star Hotel was simple yet profound: to redefine the meaning of luxury in India by offering an elevated hospitality 
                        experience. With a blend of traditional Indian hospitality and contemporary luxury, every Sagi 5-Star Hotel offers more than just a place to stay 
                        — it provides an experience that guests will cherish forever.
                    Our Expanding Legacy: Hotels Across India
                        Today, Sagi 5-Star Hotel has expanded with 6 locations in key cities across India, each offering the highest level of service, luxury, and comfort:
                        1.Sagi Mumbai – Opened in 2005:
                        2.Sagi Delhi – Opened in 2007:
                        3.Sagi Bangalore – Opened in 2011:
                        4.Sagi Jaipur – Opened in 2013:
                        5.Sagi Goa – Opened in 2015:
                        6.Sagi Kolkata – Opened in 2018:
